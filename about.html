About RTX Computers

Our Story

Started in 2020 by a group of hardware enthusiasts, RTX Computers was born out of a simple need: a store that understands gamers. We grew from a small workshop into the region's most trusted name for high-end custom builds.

Vision & Message

Our vision is to make high-performance computing accessible to everyone. We believe in transparency, education, and quality. We don't just sell parts; we sell the experience of building your dream setup.
